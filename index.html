About us

As students of the University of Michigan, we strive to ensure everybody has a voice in our society and advocate for their beliefs. Our community events include the following:

  • Tournament for High School students in our campus
  • Collegiate competitions across the nation
  • YouTube tutorials for speaking events
  • Workshops aimed at improving public speaking
