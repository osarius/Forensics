About us

As students of the University of Michigan, we strive to ensure everybody has a voice in our society and advocate for their beliefs. Here's how we do that:

  • Hold community events that aim to improve our public speaking skills while also having fun.
  • Connect and compete with other collegiate Forensics teams across the nation.
  • Organize on-campus tournaments for high school students.
  • Develop resources to make effective public speaking more accessible for high school students.
  • Build new friendships and learn from each other.
